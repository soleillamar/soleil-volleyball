Early Facts

Volleyball was first called Mintonette.

Volleyball was onced played with a basketball, then basketball bladder, now it is played with a lightweight volleyball.

Game Facts

Volleyball is now being played on the snow. It was added to the 2018 Winter Olympics and has three players on each team.

Volleyball was invented in 1895

Three-hit rule was only introduced in 1920.

First beach volleyball game was in 1930.

First olympic match was in 1964.

Cool Facts 😎

Volleyball players on average jump about 300 times per match

Volleyball is the 2nd most popular game in the world.

The longest volleyball game took 75 hours and 30 minutes.

The world record for fastest volleyball serve is 143 kph or about 82 mph.

Highest vertical jump in volleyball is 50 inches.
