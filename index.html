What Is Volleyball? 🏐

Volleyball is a team sport where teams of six players are separated by a net. Teams score points when the opposing team grounds a ball, hits a ball out, or fails to hit the ball over the net.

Serving, receiving, setting, and spiking are the main fundamentals of volleyball.

The Game

Beach Volleyball
  • Best of three sets (2/3) to 21 wins
  • Final set is played to 15 points
  • Must win with a two-point advantage
  • Switch courts every 7 points in a set
  • Players need to rotate position and server
Indoor Volleyball
  • Best of five sets (3/5) to 25 wins
  • Final set is played to 15 points
  • Must win with a two-point advantage
  • Switch courts every different set
  • Players do not rotate, but serving roatation is necessary
